This folder contains the data to train a nnUNet NN for 171A and 193A. The original data can be found in the Following folders in the Dataset171_SDO_AIA folder Under the labels:
XXX_test
XXX_train


The masks used are spoca

For the pixel value in the masks:
0: Background
1: Active regions
2: Coronal Holes

This is a single wavelength multi label data set.
